Hello, Hawke's Bay

Project Overview

The aim of this project is to create a well-defined app for booking accommodation in Hawke’s bay. The app will show the suitable accommodation list based on users’ information such as the number of guests and the night they would like to stay. Additionally, they can choose the meal options before they purchase.

• Making online accommodation booking using developing an application.
• To provide information and the ability to search for suitable accommodation for users.
• To provide the opportunity for customers to choose meal options when they choose their accommodation.

I was responsible for all the User Research, Personas, Wireframes, Prototype, UI Design and Development (HTML, CSS, JS).
