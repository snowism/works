the Little Pumpkin

Project Overview

The client is a party planner especially for children. They plan various parties such as a birthday, sleepover or celebration occasions for people from infants to teenagers. This project will implement a CMS custom theme on Wordpress with decent design to help the client edit/update their information more easily. Also, 3 breakpoints responsiveness will be efficient to achieve their business goal.

I was responsible for all the User Research, Personas, Wireframes, Prototype, UI Design and Development.
