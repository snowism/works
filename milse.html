Milse

Project Overview

The client, Milse has been established in 2013 at Britomart in New Zealand as the city’s first dessert-only fine dining restaurant. Milse does not have their own webpage at the moment. It means they do not have to offer any services like booking a table or ordering food through the internet. Nowadays, as the technology has been developing, conducting digital marketing and providing service by any devices have strong key points for running business. This project making their own application to be implemented design and functions will help to achieve their business goals.

• Increasing the number of regular and loyalty customers
• Increasing amount of selling products
• Being able to keep and manage customers’ database

I was responsible for all the User Research, Personas, Wireframes, Prototype, UI Design and Development (HTML, CSS, JS).
